Before you begin
Understanding your symptoms starts here
These four questionnaires each measure a different aspect of complex chronic illness — fatigue severity, post-viral functional status, ME/CFS symptom burden, and autonomic dysfunction. Together they build a picture of your dominant symptom pattern.

Each tool is validated and used by healthcare professionals in clinical practice. Completing them gives you structured, evidence-based language to describe what you are experiencing — and something concrete to bring to your doctor.

They do not diagnose. But they give you and your clinician a clearer starting point.
Important — please read first
These tools do not diagnose. Depression, anxiety, thyroid problems, anaemia, and other treatable conditions produce similar symptoms. If you have not yet seen a doctor, please do that first. This assessment is for people who have already done so and are still without answers.
A note on honesty
These tools are only useful if you answer honestly. Before each question ask yourself — is this genuinely different from how I was before? Is it consistent over months, not just a bad week? If the honest answer is no — answer no. Honest answers give useful results. Inflated answers mislead you.
